Summary

When the menace known as the Joker wreaks havoc and chaos on the people of Gotham, Batman must accept one of the greatest psychological and physical tests of his ability to fight injustice. The caped crusader faces his greatest challenge yet as he confronts a criminal mastermind who wants to prove that even the most noble hero can be corrupted.
